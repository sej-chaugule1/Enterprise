Why Extreme Education?


If you want your child to be best prepared to succeed both at school and beyond, Extreme Education learning systems make all the difference.

Extreme Education is a strong, flexible and dynamic learning system that focuses on the key Mathematics and English learning outcomes for the Australian Curriculum.


We believe that every child is different, and so their programme of study should be too. Tailored programs for each child allow them to progress at their own pace, reaching advanced levels and achieving success.

Our aim

Our aim is to help students become more successful, strategic and independent learners, with a positive attitude to study.
